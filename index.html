The Open Source Remote Learning Initiative

OSREI or the Open Source Remote Education Initiative is a project that plans to make available electronically aided education to underprivileged children across the world to improve their standard of education. In certain remote regions across the world, children have poor access to good teachers, good advisors and good information. Further a lot of them recieve little to no non-academic education such as sex education, moral education, international exposure etcetera. Meanwhile, schools in cities often have electronically aided education while simultaneosly having excellent staff to help their students. Electronic aided education is least existent in places where it is most necessary, in areas where high quality educational resources are scarce. We use modern Android based tablets to facilitate Minimally Invasive Education (MIE) in these areas. These devices are connected onto our global network of devices through which we constantly make available quality learning content of both academic and non-academic nature for these students.
